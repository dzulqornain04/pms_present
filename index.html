Challenges

Balancing site flexibility with system standardization.

Different Site Needs

Each site has unique processes — harder to standardize across the board.

Custom Reports

Variations in reporting requirements increase maintenance effort.

Manual Validation

Data checking is still partially manual — validation can take longer.

Support Tracking

Without a structured system, issue history and follow-ups can be missed.

Legacy Technology

Older components need upgrade for security, performance, and maintainability.

Current Challenges

Balancing site flexibility with system standardization.

Different Site Needs

Each site has unique processes — harder to standardize across the board.

Custom Reports

Variations in reporting requirements increase maintenance effort.

Manual Validation

Data checking remains partially manual — validation takes longer.

Support Tracking

Without structure, issue history and follow-ups can be missed.

Legacy Technology

Older components need upgrade for security and maintainability.
